## TL;DR Emerging YouTubers are not given the chance because YouTube's algorithm favors the bigger, more popular YouTubers.

If a person wants to find a way to make a living off a YouTube channel, they will find that the first several videos they put out will often vanish in the YouTube void. It takes a creative plan and tons of determination to even have the ability to monetize videos.
